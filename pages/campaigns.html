About Our Capaign

The Global Awareness focus areas include wildlife and environment, human happiness, companion animals, climate change and biodiversity, animal use in laboratories, and land use and food systems.

Our global awareness campaignaims to educate and engage communities around the world on critical issues affecting our planet, such as climate change,poverty,and social justice.

We believe that informed and motivated individuals can make a significant impact on global challenges.

Our Goals

  • Raise awareness about global issues.

  • Promote sustainable practices.

  • Encourage community involvement.

  • Advocate for policy changes.

What You Can Do

  • Participate in local and global events.

  • Share informatuon with your community.

  • Make lifestyle changes to reduce your environmental impact.
